Contacts

Here are some contacts and resources that can provide support and information related to Autism Spectrum Disorder:

  • Local Support Groups: Connect with local support groups for individuals with ASD and their families. These groups often provide valuable resources, events, and opportunities for networking.
  • Healthcare Professionals: Reach out to healthcare professionals who specialize in autism, including developmental pediatricians, psychologists, speech therapists, and occupational therapists.
  • Educational Institutions: Contact schools, colleges, and universities that offer specialized programs and support services for individuals with ASD.
  • Advocacy Organizations: Explore advocacy organizations dedicated to promoting awareness, acceptance, and support for individuals with ASD. These organizations may offer resources, advocacy services, and community events.
  • Government Agencies: Seek information and support from government agencies that focus on disability rights, education, and healthcare services for individuals with ASD.
